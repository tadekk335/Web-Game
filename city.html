You and the wizard leave the swamps, in front of you there is a signpost with the description "Kraster-City" pointing to the north. You go in that direction and end up in the suburbs, local residents are watching you suspiciously ...

Suddenly your road is blocked by thugs, you can see them also on the left and right side ... they surround you...

You and the wizard defeat the bandits by working together. You follow your guide Adam to Castle the city of Kraster.

Approach the draw gate, suddenly the guards run up to you full of joy. It turns out that Adam is the son of the king of the city of Kraster. They lead you to the king himself.

When you tell him about ancient artifacts, he looks at his sword which turns out to be one of them. As a reward for saving his son, he gives it to you.

You finally get an ancient artifact ...

Your adventure ends at this point.

Congratulations.
